📊Dataset/Domain
The dataset contains aviation industry records for flights such as Unique flight number, Date of flight, Route, Geographic zone (Domestic, International), Number of passengers on the flight, Total ticket sales for the flight (USD), Total departure delay in minutes, Net profit from the flight (USD).

📊 Key Insights from the Dashboard
• Total Revenue: 12M
• Total Flights: 187
• Delayed Flights: 185
• Passenger Load: 191.09
• Average Arrival Delay: 89.74 minutes

🔎 What the Dashboard Shows
• Revenue trends by day and month indicate that performance is stronger in the early months of the year compared to the later months.
• Overall, 99% of flights are delayed across regions, and international flights show the highest delay rate.
• Monthly profit analysis reveals that domestic routes consistently generate higher profits compared to international routes.
• Some routes contribute significantly more profit, helping identify high-value routes for airline operations. Certain routes such as DFW–SEA and SFO–TYO contribute significantly to overall profit.
